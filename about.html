About

We are a company that specializes in Java and Open Source development. Our niche is helping companies optimize their development process through consultation and example. We are also experts in developing web applications, web services, and micro-service architectures for mobile, office, or network consumption.

CI Wise Inc. was formed on March 19, 2015 by David L. Whitehurst and Patricia R. Whitehurst as a corporation with the state of North Carolina. The company is located just south of downtown Raleigh, North Carolina. And, while the Raleigh/Durham/Research-Triangle area continues to be a technological growth area, CI Wise aims to provide leadership in the technology space here.
